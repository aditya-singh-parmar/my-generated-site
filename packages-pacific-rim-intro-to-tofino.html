Afterwards, back to rest and relaxation for the adventures to come.</p> </div><div class="gdlr-item gdlr-content-item"><h5>Day 2:</h5> <p>Tofino is the surf capital of Canada: It’s only natural the first full day kicks off with giving it a try.
